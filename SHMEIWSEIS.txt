	Estw metablhth X(ij)(i = 1..n ,j = 1..m) dhladh metablhtes einai oles oi  energeies gia kathe ergasia 
	
Domains :
	Domain(ij) = {1..n*m} to domain einai h seira ths energeias dhladh an X(ij) = 15 tote h energeia ayth tha ektelestei dekathpempth
	

Periorismoi:

	prepei kathe lista metablhtwn Xi na einai SORTED dhladh prepei kathe energeia j na ekteleite afou ektelestoyn oi proigoumenes
an X = [
[1,5,10],
[2,3,6]
]
